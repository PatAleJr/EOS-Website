What is this?

The electron orbital simulator (EOS) is a Unity program that serves as a visual aid for learning the structure of the atom.

On the left is a 3D model of the atom selected in the periodic table. Using the dropdown, you may view a simplified model of it's electron cloud or a 3D representation of the bohr model.

On the right is the electron configuration for the corresponding element. In electron cloud view, you may click on boxes to view individual orbitals.

Development and future updates

PatAle made this for his Chemistry teacher in high school so it may be used as a learning tool in class. Since PatAle found quantum mechanics so interesting, the EOS became a larger personal project. There is currently an update in development which will provide accurate visualization of orbitals by plotting a 3D cloud of points generated using wave functions.
